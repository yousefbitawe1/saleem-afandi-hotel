A Heritage of Refined Hospitality

Saleem Afandi Hotel is a distinguished landmark of hospitality in the heart of Nablus, Palestine. We offer our guests a unique blend of authentic warmth and modern comfort, creating an atmosphere where every detail is carefully considered.

From our elegantly appointed rooms to our welcoming coffee shop and cozy seating areas, we are dedicated to providing a relaxing and memorable experience for every guest — whether for business, leisure, or family stays.
